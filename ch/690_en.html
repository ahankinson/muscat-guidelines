Catalogue name (690 $a)

The abbreviation for a thematic catalogue is given here. Entries are linked with the authority file.

New catalogues may be entered only after their approval by the RISM Zentralredaktion.

Number/page (690 $n)

This field specifies the number or the reference to a page in the thematic catalogue. Generally, the original formatting of each thematic catalogue is maintained.

If the source contains only one movement of a work, the movement number is entered after a slash.

A composition not included in the thematic catalogue is indicated with the term "deest".
