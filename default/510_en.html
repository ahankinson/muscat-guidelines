Parallel form of name (MARC 510 $a; ISDIAH 5.1.3)

This field records the various forms in which the authorised form of name of an institution with archival holdings occurs in other languages or script form(s).

Enter official names of the institution in other languages. An English version of the name can also be given as a parallel form of name. Enter each name on a separate line.

In the case of a private collection, the information “Private Collection” will be given, separated by a comma.

Examples:
The National Library of Wales [for Llyfrgell Genedlaethol Cymru]
Norwegian State Academy of Music, The Library [for Norges musikkhøgskole, Biblioteket]
ERESBIL - Archivo Vasco de la Música [for ERESBIL - Musikaren Euskal Artxiboa]
ERESBIL - Basque Archives of Music [for ERESBIL - Musikaren Euskal Artxiboa]
