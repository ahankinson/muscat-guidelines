Other shelfmark (591)
Enter other shelfmarks (also called call numbers) here. Any shelfmarks that are currently valid should be entered here. For example, some operas have separate shelfmarks for every act, or some partbooks have individual shelfmarks for each partbook. Enter shelfmarks in the manner indicated for the field Shelfmark (852 $p) above.

Shelfmarks that are no longer in use should be entered in the field Former shelfmark (olim) (852 $d).
