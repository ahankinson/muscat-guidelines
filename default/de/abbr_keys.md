Major (mode): A, B, C, D, E, F, G, C|x, D|x, F|x, G|x, A|b, B|b, C|b, D|b, E|b, G|b &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;

Minor (mode): a, b, c, d, e, f, g, a|x, d|x, f|x, g|x, a|b, b|b, c|b, d|b, e|b, g|b

&nbsp;

_Examples_:

- A = A major
- B = B major
- b = B minor
- f|x = F-sharp minor
- B|b = B-flat major
