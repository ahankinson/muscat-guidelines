Additional personal name (700)

This part records the editors of the item, additional people involved in the publication, and composers or other people that the publication is about.


Name (700 $a)

Enter the name. This field is linked to the Personal names authority file.

Function (700 $4)

Only select from the following:

  • Additional author [aut]
  • Editor [edt]
  • Composer [cmp]
  • Associated name [asn]: such as honoree, name in title, collector, text author, other, etc.
