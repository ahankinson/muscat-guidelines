Standardized titles

Printed music bears distinctive titles much more frequently than music manuscripts do, and moreover they are frequently cited in the literature as such. Please observe these rules for the field Standardized title (240) when cataloging printed music.

For guidance, refer to standard reference works such as Grove and MGG, and authority files from the Library of Congress or VIAF.

1. Spelling

Enter standardized titles using modern orthography, but do not overcorrect archaic usage or dialect/regionalisms. Frequent changes include v to u, i to j, y to i, and uu to w. The Central Office can assist with unfamiliar foreign languages. Additional variations can be entered in the field Additional title (730).

Examples:
  • RISM A/I: RR 3030c
    Title on source: Musicalische Grab=schrifft.
    Standardized title: Musikalische Grabschrift

  • RISM A/I: T 848
    Title on source: Hochzeit=Lied
    Standardized title: Hochzeitslied

2. Deriving a standardized title

Standardized titles should consist of the title of the print up to a natural break, frequently a comma, period, or a statement of the author, instrumentation, number, or imprint. Sometimes a distinctive title appears on a page other than the title page.

Examples:
  • RISM A/I: B 841
    Title on source: L'Organo Suonarino...Opera terza decima
    Standardized title: L'organo suonarino

  • RISM A/I: B 805
    Title on source: Vezzo di perle musicali modernamente conteste alla regia sposa effigiata nella sacra cantica; opera ventesima terza
    Standardized title: Vezzo di perle musicali

  • RISM A/I: B 3781
    Title on source: Novo giardino de concerti a quattro voci, per cantare a due chori con due voci [...]
    Standardized title:  Novo giardino de concerti

  • RISM A/I: B 5024
    Title on source: Lamentationi per la settima santa, a quattro voci, con un Benedictus a cinque, e [...]
    Standardized title:  Lamentationi per la settima santa

  • RISM no. 1000000056
    Title on source: CANTO | CANTICI SPIRITVALI | A VNA, DVE, TRE, QVATTRO | CINQVE, ET SEI VOCI
    Standardized title: Cantici spirituali

  • RISM no. 1000000060
    Title on source: SONATE | CONCERTATE | In Stil Moderno, per Sonar nel Organo [...]
    Standardized title: Sonate concertate in stil moderno

  • RISM A/I: RR 3030a
    Title page: Kurtze Beschreibung | Deß | über den höchstbetraurlichen frühzeitigen | tödtlichen Hintritt | Des weiland | Durchleuchtigsten [...]
    Title above first song: Trawr=und Klag=Lied | über den hochschmertzlichen tödtlichen Hintritt | Ihr. Kön: Maytt: | unsers allergnädigsten Königs und Herrn/
    Standardized title: Trauer- und Klaglied


3. Libro/Buch/Teil/part, etc.

If the title indicates that the item has an affiliation with another item, such as libro/Buch/livre or Teil/part, enter the title with the component in the original language (but standardized spelling) and the arabic number following the title.

Examples:
  • RISM A/I: P 687
    Title on source: CANTVS | IOANNIS PETRALOYSII | PRAENESTINI | Missarum cum quatuor, quinque, & sex vocibus. | LIBER DVODECIMVS. | Nunc primum in lucem editus.
    Standardized title: Missarum, liber 12

  • RISM A/I: B 4528
    Title on source: A second book of new anthems and psalm tunes.
    Standardized title: New anthems and psalm tunes, book 2

  • RISM A/I: C 2167
    Title on source: Motecta quae binis, ternis, quaternis vocibus concinuntur ... una cum basso ad organum, liber secundus
    Standardized title: Motecta, liber 2

  • RISM A/I: L 2044
    Title on source: Second livre de guiterre [!], contenant plusieurs chansons en forme de voix [!] de ville: nouvellement remises en tabulature
    Standardized title: Livre de guiterre, no. 2

  • RISM A/I: SS 6593a
    Title on source: Ander Theil | Der Preussischen | Fest-Lieder/ | Von Ostern an biß Advent | Mit 5/ 6/ 7/ 8. Stimmen.
    Standardized title: Preussische Festlieder, Teil 2

  • RISM B/I: 1539|27
    Title on source: Ein ausszug guter alter und new | er Teutscher liedlein, einer rechten Teutschen art, | auff allerley Instrumenten zubrauchen,
    Standardized title: Frische teutsche Liedlein, Teil 1
    Additional title: Ein Ausszug guter alter und neuer teutscher Liedlein


4. One genre as title

If the title consists of only one genre and there is no indication of being a book or part in a series, enter the number of pieces contained in the item followed by the genre.

For earlier imprints (up to around 1700), use the genre as written on the item. As we move further into the 18th century and printed music proliferates, genres are increasingly used as titles and titles become less and less distinctive. For later imprints, use the genre in English.

Examples:
  • RISM A/I: G 3427
    Title on source: Motetti a cinque voci, con le letanie della Beata Vergine
    Standardized title: 16 Motetti
    Note that the item is from 1614 and is cited in Grove, MGG, and the Library of Congress as "Motetti."

  • RISM A/I: P 3789
    Title on source: Douze Grandes | SONATES | Pour Forte-Piano | Avec Accompagnement de Violon et Violoncelle
    Standardized title: 3 Trios
    Note that the item is from ca. 1799, contains 3 trios, and is classified as a trio in Benton's Pleyel catalog, Grove, and MGG.

5. More than one genre as title

Titles consisting of multiple genres should be treated as distinctive titles.

Examples:
  • RISM AI/: B 3753
    Title on source: Lamentationi, Miserere et Improperii a quattro voci, per la settimana santa, con il basso per l'organo.
    Standardized title: Lamentationi, miserere et improperii

  • RISM A/I: C 906
    Title on source: Motetti et dialoghi a cinque, sei, sette [...]
    Standardized title: Motetti et dialoghi

6. No logical title

If no logical title can be discerned from the source, derive the standardized title from the following in this order of preference: text incipit, genre, tempo indication.

Example:
  • RISM A/I: T 293
    Title on source: A.Z. | der besuchte JaskenThal | dem | Vielbelobten | Paare/ | Hn. Gottlieb Hupplis | und | Jungfrauen | Anna Czirlinskin | Des | Weyland Ehrenvesten | Herrn Hanß Czirlinskin | hinterlassenen Ehelichen Tochter | als dasselbe am 13. des Brachmonats am Tage Tobiae | im 1661. [...]
    Standardized title from text incipit: Dir schenkt Gott Lieb Gottes Wille
