Cataloging collections

When cataloging collections, please note that:

  1. The collection main entry (parent record) should contain information that applies to the entire collection.
  2. Any information that is relevant only for some parts of the collection should be entered in the individual entry.

If the works in the collection have different composers, scoring, etc., then do not enter them in the parent record.

For collections in which the works are very similar, it is a good strategy to create a full first entry and use the "Duplicate" function to copy the record. Then for the following records you only have to change fields such as title and page number.

Collections may be made up of separate collections.

Cataloging pasticcios and compilations

Pasticcios and compilations are cataloged as collections. Each part is entered separately as an individual record in a collection. More detailed guidelines on this will follow.
