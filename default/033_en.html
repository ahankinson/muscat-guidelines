Any dates that appear on the source should be entered here.

 

Date type (033)

Select what type of date you are entering.

  • Single date
  • Multiple single dates
  • Range of dates

 

Date (033 $a)

Enter different types of dates as follows:

  • Single date: ????YYYYMMDD
  • Multiple single dates: yyyymmdd, yyyymmdd, yyyymmdd, etc.
  • Range of dates: yyyymmdd-yyyymmdd

 

Example:

Multiple single dates: 18791213, 18791216, 18791223

 

If some parts of the date are missing, use a hyphen.

Example:
179112-- for December 1791

 

Other dates may be indicated as on the source.

Example:

Easter 1744
