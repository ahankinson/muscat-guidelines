Note on content (505 $a)

This field can contain additional information on the contents of the source which do not fit into any of the given categories. The entries are in the cataloguing language.

E.g.:

  • Information on different versions of the work
  • Information on the structure of collections
  • Number of verses in strophic songs
  • General structural information about the source
  • Information on the complete scoring, if the material is incomplete
