Preceding entry (780 $w)

This field and the next field (Succeeding entry) are used to record and link alternate names of items, such as a journal title that has changed names.

Enter here the name that the periodical was formerly known as. This field is linked to the Secondary literature database.

Example: The journal The Viola da Gamba Society Journal began in 2007 and was preceded by Chelys. The Journal of the Viola da Gamba Society (published from 1969 to 2004). In the record for The Viola da Gamba Society Journal, enter the following:
Preceding entry (780 $w): Chelys. The Journal of the Viola da Gamba Society
