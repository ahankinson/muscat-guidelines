Item part of (760)

Use this field to link your item to the yearbook, periodical, or book that the item is a part of.

Title of periodical or book (760 $t)

Enter the title of the periodical or book. This field is linked to the Secondary literature database. Titles not yet in the database can be added.


Volume, year, page (760 $g)

Enter as appropriate the volume, issue, year, and/or page numbers for the item. For example, for a periodical enter the volume, year, and page numbers. Enter in a form appropriate to the style of the journal.

Examples:
23 (1986), p. 5-10
2, 35 (2014), 7-16
71 (1997), Heft 5, p.275-292
vol. 3/1-2; 11/1-2

For a chapter within a collection of essays, enter the page numbers of the chapter:
Example:
p. 76-109

In general, make sure to include enough information so that someone else would be able to find the item themselves.
