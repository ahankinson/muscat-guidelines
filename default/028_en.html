Plate number (028 $a)

This field is for plate numbers, which typically appear on printed items but can also be found on manuscripts that were copied from a printed music edition.

Enter the plate number exactly as it appears on the source.

Examples:
B. & H. 8533
S. B. 2. 11
424
L. M. 2. A. 31

Publisher's numbers must be entered in the field General note (500).
