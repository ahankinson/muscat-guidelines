Text (740 $a)

Texts entered in the music incipit need for technical reasons to be repeated here, in standardised spelling.

Also, passages from libretti and theoretical texts have to be mentioned here.

The field is repeatable.

Entered data is retrieved from the authority file. New entries are possible.
