Place of an event (651 $a)

The place of a performance is indicated if it appears on the source. It must correspond with information in the field "Date (033 $a)". The information is standardised and is linked with the authority file. The field is repeatable.

When entering new geographical names in the authority file, please notice that these are preferrably entered in the local language.

E.g.:

  • Praha
  • Milano
